Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Overview

Autoshorts Agent Factory is a desktop application and workflow used to create and prepare short-form video content. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when users operate the software and connect third-party publishing platforms.

Information processed by the app

The application may process the following categories of information:

  • User-provided video project files, text prompts, scripts, and media assets.
  • Locally generated output files such as captions, thumbnails, metadata, and rendered videos.
  • OAuth credentials and tokens required to connect user-owned publishing accounts.
  • Operational logs needed to diagnose generation and publishing steps.

How information is used

Information is used to:

  • Create, edit, and export short-form video content.
  • Generate descriptions, titles, captions, and other publishing metadata.
  • Authenticate the user with supported social platforms through official authorization flows started by the desktop app.
  • Upload or prepare content for publication when the user explicitly requests it.

Local storage

The application is designed to operate primarily on the user's local machine. Project artifacts, tokens, and logs may be stored locally so the workflow can continue across sessions.

Website role

This website is a public information page for the desktop application. It is used for policy publication, support contact details, and platform review context. The main product workflow, including account connection and content preparation, happens in the desktop environment rather than on this website.

Third-party services

Users may connect third-party providers, including AI services, media providers, and social platforms. When these integrations are used, relevant data may be transmitted to those services under the user's direction and according to the terms and privacy policies of those providers.

Account connections

When a user connects a publishing platform account, the app stores the resulting authorization token locally to support future publishing actions. Users are responsible for protecting access to the device where these tokens are stored.

Data sharing

The application is not intended to sell personal data. Information is shared only as needed to perform requested actions, such as generating content or sending a video to an authorized platform under the user's control.

Retention

Data retention depends on how long files, logs, and tokens remain on the user's local machine or in the user's chosen infrastructure. Users may delete local artifacts and revoke connected platform access at any time.
