Desktop Video Workflow

Create and prepare short-form videos for social publishing.

Autoshorts Agent Factory is a desktop tool that helps create short vertical videos with AI-assisted topic research, scripting, voice generation, subtitles, thumbnails, and publishing-ready metadata.

View Privacy Policy View Terms

What the app does

  • Generates short-form video concepts and scripts
  • Builds subtitles, thumbnails, and platform metadata
  • Prepares exports for YouTube and TikTok workflows
  • Lets the user review content before final publishing

How it works

The application runs on the user's desktop and processes local project files to generate video packages. It can assemble narration, subtitles, and visual assets, then export a final video file with platform-ready descriptions and titles.

For supported social platforms, the app can connect the user's own account through official authorization flows and help send prepared content for publishing or final review.

Intended use

This service is designed for creators, editors, and channel operators who want to streamline short-video production while keeping human review in the loop.

Users remain responsible for reviewing their content, media rights, descriptions, and publishing decisions before a video goes live on any platform.
